[h1]Unique Meat Flavors[/h1]

Every new game randomly assigns a flavor to each animal species in your world — vanilla, DLC, and modded animals included. When a colonist eats meat (raw or in a meal), they gain an extra mood thought based on the species' flavor.

[b]Five tiers:[/b]
[list]
[*][b]Bland[/b] – mood -3
[*][b]Common[/b] – silent (most species)
[*][b]Tasty[/b] – mood +2
[*][b]Delicious[/b] – mood +4
[*][b]Exquisite[/b] – mood +6
[/list]

The distribution is centered on [b]Common[/b], with rare extremes. Flavors are saved per game, so a Thrumbo that's exquisite in one playthrough might be bland in the next.

[h1]Features[/h1]
[list]
[*]Per-save randomization
[*]Inspect strings show the flavor on every meat stack
[*]Meals with mixed meat average their flavors (non-meat ingredients ignored)
[*]Vanilla, DLC, and modded animals all supported
[*]Mid-campaign safe — add an animal mod later, its meats get flavors at next load
[*]Bilingual: English / Spanish
[*][url=https://steamcommunity.com/sharedfiles/filedetails/?id=2606448745]Multiplayer[/url] compatible — settings are host-authoritative, re-rolls and toggles sync to all clients
[/list]

[h1]Settings[/h1]
[list]
[*][b]Cosmetic-only mode[/b] – flavors still randomize and show on inspect strings, but eating doesn't generate mood thoughts.
[*][b]Magnitude multiplier[/b] – 0.5× / 1× / 2×, applies forward to newly-gained thoughts.
[*][b]Re-roll all flavors[/b] – reassigns every flavor in the current save (confirmation gated).
[/list]

[h1]Compatibility[/h1]
[list]
[*]RimWorld 1.6 + all vanilla DLCs (Royalty, Ideology, Biotech, Anomaly, Odyssey)
[*]Animal mods that follow the standard meatDef pattern (Vanilla Animals Expanded, Alpha Animals, Megafauna, etc.)
[*][url=https://steamcommunity.com/sharedfiles/filedetails/?id=2009463077]Harmony[/url] – required dependency
[*][url=https://steamcommunity.com/sharedfiles/filedetails/?id=2606448745]Multiplayer[/url] – optional, fully synced if present
[*]Save-game safe — adding or removing the mod doesn't break existing saves
[/list]

[h1]Sabores Únicos de Carne (Español)[/h1]

Cada partida nueva asigna aleatoriamente un sabor a cada especie animal de tu mundo — vanilla, DLCs y mods incluidos. Cuando un colonista come carne (cruda o en un meal), recibe un thought extra de humor según el sabor de la especie.

[b]Cinco niveles:[/b]
[list]
[*][b]Insípido[/b] – humor -3
[*][b]Común[/b] – silencioso (la mayoría de especies)
[*][b]Sabroso[/b] – humor +2
[*][b]Delicioso[/b] – humor +4
[*][b]Exquisito[/b] – humor +6
[/list]

La distribución está centrada en [b]Común[/b], con extremos raros. Los sabores se guardan por partida, así que un Thrumbo exquisito en una partida puede ser insípido en la siguiente.

[h1]Características[/h1]
[list]
[*]Re-randomización en cada partida nueva
[*]Inspect strings muestran el sabor en cada stack de carne
[*]Meals con carnes mezcladas promedian sus sabores (los ingredientes no-carne se ignoran)
[*]Vanilla, DLCs y mods de animales — todos soportados
[*]Seguro mid-campaign — agregá un mod de animales después y sus carnes obtienen sabor en el próximo load
[*]Bilingüe: Inglés / Español
[*]Compatible con [url=https://steamcommunity.com/sharedfiles/filedetails/?id=2606448745]Multiplayer[/url] — los settings los manda el host, re-rolls y toggles se sincronizan a todos los clientes
[/list]

[h1]Configuración[/h1]
[list]
[*][b]Modo solo cosmético[/b] – los sabores se randomizan y se ven en el inspect, pero comer no genera thoughts de humor.
[*][b]Multiplicador de magnitud[/b] – 0.5× / 1× / 2×, se aplica a thoughts que se ganan a partir del cambio.
[*][b]Re-randomizar todos los sabores[/b] – reasigna todos los sabores de la partida (con confirmación).
[/list]

[h1]Compatibilidad[/h1]
[list]
[*]RimWorld 1.6 + todos los DLCs vanilla (Royalty, Ideology, Biotech, Anomaly, Odyssey)
[*]Mods de animales que siguen el patrón estándar de meatDef (Vanilla Animals Expanded, Alpha Animals, Megafauna, etc.)
[*][url=https://steamcommunity.com/sharedfiles/filedetails/?id=2009463077]Harmony[/url] – dependencia requerida
[*][url=https://steamcommunity.com/sharedfiles/filedetails/?id=2606448745]Multiplayer[/url] – opcional, totalmente sincronizado si está presente
[*]Seguro al guardar — agregar o quitar el mod no rompe partidas existentes
[/list]

NOTES FOR PUBLISHING:

- Steam Workshop title (max ~100 chars, will appear at the top):
    Unique Meat Flavors

- Suggested tags (Steam dropdown when uploading):
    Mood, Animal, Cooking, 1.6

- Preview image (640×360) goes at About/Preview.png. Steam pulls it
  from the upload automatically.

- The description above is what to paste into the Workshop page's
  description box. The About.xml description is what RimWorld's
  upload tool puts there initially — it'll already be there in
  plain text, but pasting the BBCode version replaces it with the
  richer formatted version.

- After publishing, RimWorld writes About/PublishedFileId.txt with
  the Workshop file ID. That file is gitignored so your local copy
  stays clean of personal Steam IDs.
